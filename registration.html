Affiliate membership

Day membership or annual membership is required.

SEORC is an affiliate of Australian Extreme Obstacle Racing. All participants must complete a day membership form or become an annual member before taking part in an event.

Before You Register

  • Check your AEORA annual or day membership is current
  • Have rider, horse, and emergency contact details ready
  • Use rider surname and event date as the payment reference
  • Bring an approved helmet and safe riding gear on the day
